Who we are

In the year of 2007 the three friends, Markus, Jonathan and Jonas sat in their favourite couch, staring plainlessly into the wall, when suddenly, Jonathan said: "The world is so messed up." Startled and a bit confused his two friends stared at him, but remained in silence. A few moments later Jonas said: "Someone should do something about it." Markus and Jonathan hummed in agreement, sighed and then continued to stare into the wall.

"Someone should do something about it."

Throughout the years the friends continued to have this thought that someone ought to do something about the world, but never actually doing or thinking anything past that. Ten years later, in 2017 they sat in that same sofa again, staring plainlessly into the wall, when Jonathan said: "The world is so messed up". Again, his two friends were confused but this time it was because they were thinking exactly the same thing. Even though they had all been in this spot ten years earlier, something was different. They jumped up from the couch and shouted in unison: "We should do something about it!" Ever since, the three friends have worked tirelessly day and night, to give you the best products this world has ever seen.

"We should do something about it!"

As you might understand from this story, it took us a long time to wake up and realise that we, everyone in this world should do something. In our hearts we now know, that maybe we can't do everything but if everyone does something, we can make this world a better place.

"Don't try to do everything, just do something"
